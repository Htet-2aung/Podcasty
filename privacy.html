Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

At Podcasty, developed by Origins, we believe that your listening habits should remain private. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide a premium podcast experience:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your name and email address via Supabase to sync your library across devices.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data regarding app performance and crash logs to improve stability.
  • Local Data: Podcasty stores downloaded episodes locally on your device storage to enable offline playback.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized features like your "Followed Shows" and "Listening History".
  • To facilitate Over-The-Air (OTA) updates through EAS to ensure you have the latest features.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our authentication systems via Supabase.

3. Third-Party Services

Podcasty utilizes trusted third-party providers to enhance functionality:

  • iTunes API: Used to search and retrieve podcast metadata. No personal info is shared with Apple during this process.
  • Supabase: Provides secure authentication and database management.
  • Expo/EAS: Used for app deployment and OTA updates.

4. Offline Storage & Permissions

Podcasty requires permission to access your device's file system to store and play downloaded audio episodes. We do not access your photos, contacts, or other personal files.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure authentication tokens, to protect your account information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time directly through the Profile section of the app.
